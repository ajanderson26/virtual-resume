ANNIKA ANDERSON

Memorandum on Rental Property Conditions

The policy memorandum on rental housing standards was a memo I wrote for a public policy class advocating for tenants rights in the City of Madison to rental housing that is safe and healthy regardless of income. The policy memo proposes policy alternatives to combat the issue of unhealthy rental housing in the City of Madison with targeted licensing programs for landlords as the ultimate solution. This memo raises important concerns over housing equity in a city struggling with the lack of affordable housing options for many low and moderate income citizens.
